1. BUSINESS OVERVIEW
-------------------
The objective of this project is to develop an interactive Power BI dashboard
to analyze credit card transactions and customer behavior. The dashboard will
support business stakeholders in understanding revenue trends, customer
segmentation, spending patterns, and card performance to enable
data-driven decision-making.

---

2. BUSINESS PROBLEM STATEMENT
-----------------------------
The organization currently lacks a centralized analytical view to:
- Monitor credit card transaction performance
- Identify high-value customers
- Understand customer spending behavior
- Track revenue and interest income trends

Manual reporting processes are time-consuming and do not provide
actionable insights in real time.

---

3. BUSINESS OBJECTIVES
----------------------
- Provide a consolidated view of credit card transactions and customer data
- Identify revenue-generating customer segments
- Analyze transaction trends by time, card type, and customer demographics
- Improve strategic decision-making through visual insights

---

4. STAKEHOLDERS
---------------
- Business Analysts
- Finance & Banking Team
- Sales & Marketing Team
- Senior Management

---

5. DATA SOURCES
---------------
The following CSV files are used as data sources:

1. credit_card.csv
   - Transaction details
   - Revenue and interest earned
   - Card category and transaction type

2. customer.csv
   - Customer demographic information
   - Gender, income group, and age details

3. cc_add.csv
   - Additional credit card attributes

4. cust_add.csv
   - Customer address and regional information

---

6. KEY METRICS (KPIs)
---------------------
- Total Transactions
- Total Revenue
- Total Interest Earned
- Average Transaction Value
- Number of Active Customers
- Revenue by Card Category
- Revenue by Customer Segment
- Transaction Growth Rate

---

7. DIMENSIONS FOR ANALYSIS
--------------------------
- Time (Year, Quarter, Month)
- Card Category
- Transaction Type
- Customer Age Group
- Gender
- Income Group
- Location / Region

---

8. DASHBOARD REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------
The Power BI dashboard should include:

A. Credit Card Transaction Dashboard
   - Total Revenue & Total Transactions
   - Interest Earned Analysis
   - Transaction Trends over Time
   - Card Category Performance
   - Transaction Type Breakdown

B. Customer Analytics Dashboard
   - Customer Demographics Overview
   - Income Group Analysis
   - High-Value Customer Identification
   - Regional Customer Distribution
   - Spending Behavior by Segment

---

9. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------
- Interactive filters and slicers
- Drill-down capability by time and category
- Dynamic KPI calculations using DAX
- Automated data refresh capability
- Clear and user-friendly visual layout

---

10. NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------
- Dashboard performance should be optimized for large datasets
- Visuals should be easy to interpret and business-friendly
- Data accuracy and consistency must be maintained
- Scalable design for future data expansion

---

11. ASSUMPTIONS
---------------
- All input CSV files contain valid and accurate data
- Data relationships are consistent across datasets
- Power BI Desktop is used for report development

---

12. EXPECTED BUSINESS OUTCOMES
-------------------------------
- Improved visibility into credit card performance
- Better identification of high-revenue customers
- Faster decision-making through real-time dashboards
- Enhanced understanding of customer behavior and trends

---

13. SUCCESS CRITERIA
--------------------
- Accurate KPI calculations
- Positive stakeholder feedback
- Reduction in manual reporting efforts
- Actionable insights derived from dashboards
